# Planets and their attributes

## Proxima Centauri B:

### Description:

#### Dusty, Mountains, High Solar wind

 - Average Temp: -38°F
 - Surface Light: No brighter than twilight
 - Mass: 1.27 Earths
 - Grav: 1.1 x Earth
 - Atmosphere: Thick Hydrogen Clouds on surface, thin atmosphere otherwise
 - Distance: 4 LY from Sol
